# Progression Rules for AI Routine Generation

## Weekly Volume Progression

- **Conservative increase**: 5-10% volume increase per week maximum
- **Deload frequency**: Every 3-4 weeks for intermediate/advanced
- **Beginner**: Linear progression preferred; add weight or reps when form allows

## Strength Progression Logic

1. **Double progression**: Increase reps within range first, then add load
2. **Load increments**: 2.5kg for upper body, 5kg for lower body
3. **Rep range completion**: If hitting top of rep range for all sets, consider load increase

## Hypertrophy Progression

1. **Volume landmarks**: Stay within MEV-MRV range
2. **Progressive overload**: Via volume (sets), load, or density
3. **Exercise rotation**: Can vary exercises every 4-6 weeks to manage fatigue

## Session Duration Constraints

- **Minimum**: 30 minutes (warm-up + 2-3 exercises)
- **Maximum**: 90 minutes (diminishing returns beyond)
- **Optimal**: 45-75 minutes for most users

## Recovery Considerations

- **48-72 hours** between training same muscle group for hypertrophy
- **24-48 hours** for strength (lighter sessions)
- **Full rest day** recommended at least 1x per week
